## What to fix (top 5, ranked by impact)

### 1. Role 2 title leaks "Scrum Master" into a PM-positioned resume
**Issue:** Tagline says "Senior Technical Product Manager." Role 1 title says "Senior Product Manager." Role 2 title says **"Lead Scrum Master & Senior Agile Coach"** — that's a positioning drift the recruiter scan will catch.
**Why it matters:** A 7-second scanner sees Title→Role1→Role2 and starts pattern-matching. If the second-most-recent role is "Scrum Master," they may bucket you as "Agile Coach who is trying to pivot to PM" instead of "Senior PM with deep delivery roots."
**Suggested fix:** Re-title Role 2 to **"Senior Agile Coach & Product Practice Lead"** or **"Lead Product Coach & Agile Transformation"** — both are truthful (you mentored Product Owners on roadmaps and EBM) and they keep the PM thread visible.

### 2. Improve So middle bullet is a kitchen-sink list
**Issue:** Current bullet 2 packs AgriGui'AI + Bip'AI + arXiv research into one line — it reads like a checklist.
**Why it matters:** Each of these three is a separate proof-point. Compressing them dilutes each one.
**Suggested fix:** Split into 2 tighter bullets, OR pick the strongest signal for HPE (the published ML research is the highest-prestige line for a Senior PM role) and lead with that. Move the WhatsApp products to one short clause.

### 3. "Earlier Experience" block undersells 7 years (2013–2020)
**Issue:** 3 roles compressed into 3 single-line entries with no metrics. For a 13-year career, that's the period that proves you've shipped Java/Spring/enterprise systems — useful technical credibility for HPE's Engineering & QA category placement.
**Why it matters:** The JD asks for "Technical understanding and knowledge of the relevant industry." Hidden technical depth is wasted depth.
**Suggested fix:** Add ONE short metric to the SOAT line (e.g., "delivered at ENEDIS, Credit Agricole, Société Générale — 100+ practitioners trained") and ONE technical clue to the Capgemini Paris line ("Java/Spring delivery at scale; founded cross-org Scrum Masters CoP with 50+ members").

### 4. Summary's first sentence could lean harder into HPE's exact language
**Issue:** Current opener: "Senior Technical Product Manager with **13+ years** driving end-to-end product strategy, operational product roadmap ownership..."
**Why it matters:** HPE's JD opens with "accelerating GreenLake Flex solutions offerings while focusing on the **end-to-end customer experience**." We have "end-to-end product strategy" — close but not the specific phrase.
**Suggested fix:** Add the words **"end-to-end customer experience"** somewhere in the summary's first 2 sentences. Truthful (Edge Platform IS a customer experience for internal labs + market teams) and it's a free verbatim match.

### 5. Competencies section is dense — flag for visual review
**Issue:** 4 categories × 8–12 items each. Reads as a wall in pure text. Renders fine in the V2 styled .docx (table rows visually segment) but could feel heavy in the ATS plain version.
**Why it matters:** Recruiter eye fatigue → less likely to read the Experience that follows.
**Suggested fix:** No content change needed. **Visually verify** in `resume-ats.docx` after open. If it looks heavy, drop 2–3 items per category (cut the most generic terms — "API-first Architectures", "CI/CD" — keep the differentiators).
